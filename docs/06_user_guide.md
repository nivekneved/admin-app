# 📘 Travel Lounge Admin Guide

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to the **Travel Lounge Admin Portal**. This strategic hub allows you to manage the entire 11/10 Elite travel ecosystem, including services, bookings, customers, and site content.

---

## 2. Key Modules

### 📊 Dashboard
The high-level overview of your business performance.
- **Analytics**: Real-time sales data and visitor trends.
- **Pending Actions**: Quick access to new bookings and inquiries needing attention.

### 💼 Service Management
Manage everything you sell to your customers.
- **Services**: Create and edit Hotels, Flights, Cruises, and Activities.
- **Categories**: Organize services into logical groups for the web and mobile apps.
- **Gallery**: Support for multi-image uploads with high-definition resolution.

### 📅 Booking & Operations
The engine of your day-to-day operations.
- **Bookings**: Manage customer reservations, update statuses, and track dates.
- **Orders**: View and manage product orders and transactions.
- **Invoices**: Generate and send professional invoices directly from the portal.

### 🖋️ Dynamic CMS (Content Management)
Full control over the look and feel of the web and mobile applications.
- **Hero Slider**: Manage the large spotlight banners on the homepage.
- **CMS Sections**: Edit homepage blocks, destination cards, and special announcements.
- **FAQs**: Maintain a searchable list of frequently asked questions.
- **Reviews**: Moderate and display customer testimonials.
- **Popup Ads**: Schedule and manage promotional popups.

### 👥 Customer & Staff Management
- **Customers**: Comprehensive database of all registered travelers.
- **Team/Staff**: Manage administrative access, roles, and permissions for your team.

### 📧 Communication & Marketing
- **Inquiries**: Handle direct messages and contact form submissions.
- **Subscribers**: Export and manage your newsletter mailing list.
- **News**: Publish blog posts and company updates to the "News" section.

### ⚙️ Global Settings
Centralized branding control.
- **Branding**: Update logos, favicons, and primary brand colors.
- **SEO & Social**: Manage metadata for search engines and social media sharing.
- **Navigation**: Use the **Navigation Manager** to edit header and footer menus.

---

## 3. Best Practices
- **Images**: Use high-resolution images for services to maintain the "Elite" aesthetic.
- **Status Updates**: Keep booking statuses updated to trigger automatic emails to customers.
- **SEO**: Always fill in the "Description" field in settings to improve your search engine rankings.
