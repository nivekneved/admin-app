# Travel Lounge Admin Panel Features

## Core Modules

- **Dashboard**: Real-time overview of bookings, revenue, and customer activity.
- **Customers**: Management of user profiles and contact history with dedicated history tracking.
- **Invoices**: Generation and management of billing documents.
- **Products & Services**: Full CRUD operations for all travel offerings (Hotels, Activities, Tours, etc.).
- **Bookings**: Automated tracking and status management for all reservations.
- **Reviews Moderation**: Portal to approve or reject customer reviews before they appear on the site.
- **FAQ Manager**: Dynamic management of frequently asked questions and categories.
- **CMS (Content Management)**: Strategic management of site-wide text (e.g., About Us) via Supabase.
- **Inquiry Inbox**: Management of customer messages from contact forms with tracking and response features.
- **Subscriber Manager**: Tools to monitor and manage newsletter growth and statuses.
- **Editorial & News**: Complete portal for publishing and archiving travel news and blog posts.
- **Team & Staff**: Management of professional profiles and administrative access.
- **Hero Slider**: Dynamic control over homepage media, CTAs, and headlines.

## Interface & Quality

- **Unified Brand Design**: Strategic use of the core red theme and charcoal palette.
- **Responsive Stacking Tables**: Mobile-optimized data presentation for effortless navigation.
- **Dynamic Frontend Sync**: Real-time integration where admin changes instantly update the `web-app`.
- **Security**: Robust Supabase RLS policies and input validation (React Hook Form + Zod).
- **Stability**: Implementation of error boundaries, code splitting, and memoized clients.
